Job Description

The Gauteng Department of Roads and Transport is seeking a reliable and experienced Driver/Messenger to join their team. The successful candidate will be responsible for transporting personnel, documents, and goods, as well as performing messenger duties to ensure the smooth operation of departmental activities.

Key Responsibilities

  • Transport personnel, documents, and goods to designated locations.
  • Ensure the safe and timely delivery of items.
  • Perform routine vehicle maintenance checks and report any issues.
  • Maintain accurate records of trips, mileage, and deliveries.
  • Adhere to all traffic laws and safety regulations.
  • Assist with general office duties as required.
  • Ensure the cleanliness and proper maintenance of the vehicle.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A valid driver's license (Code [Insert Required License Code from the Page, e.g., Code 10 or Code 14]).
  • A minimum of [Insert Years of Experience from the Page] years of driving experience.
  • Knowledge of Gauteng roads and routes.
  • Excellent driving record with no traffic violations.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work independently and under pressure.
  • South African citizenship.

Application Process

To apply for the Driver/Messenger position at the Gauteng Department of Roads and Transport, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • Certified copies of your driver's license and ID.
  • Certified copies of any relevant qualifications.
