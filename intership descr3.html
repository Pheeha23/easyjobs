Sales Graduate - Faurecia South Africa

Graduate Program

Location: Cape Town

Date Posted: 09 February 2025

Application Ends: 14 February 2025

Job Type: Full-time (Graduate Program)

Qualification: Bachelor's Degree in Sales, Marketing, Business Administration, or related field

Job Description

Faurecia South Africa is offering an exciting opportunity for a Sales Graduate to join their team. This graduate program is designed to provide recent graduates with hands-on experience in sales and business development within the automotive industry. The successful candidate will work closely with the sales team to drive business growth, build client relationships, and contribute to the achievement of sales targets.

Program Overview

The Sales Graduate program at Faurecia South Africa is a 12-month program aimed at developing future sales professionals. The graduate will be exposed to various aspects of sales, including customer relationship management, market analysis, and sales strategy development. This program offers a unique opportunity to gain industry-specific knowledge and develop essential sales skills in a global automotive company.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist the sales team in achieving sales targets and objectives.
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clients and stakeholders.
  • Conduct market research and analysis to identify new business opportunities.
  • Prepare sales reports, presentations, and proposals.
  • Support the development and implementation of sales strategies.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure customer satisfaction.
  • Participate in sales meetings, training sessions, and workshops.
  • Perform administrative tasks related to sales activities.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A Bachelor's Degree in Sales, Marketing, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Strong interest in sales and business development.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • South African citizenship is required.

Application Process

To apply for the Sales Graduate program at Faurecia South Africa, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this program.

Applications should be sent by the closing date. Applications sent after the deadline will not be considered.
