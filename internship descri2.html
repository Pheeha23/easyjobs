South African Civil Aviation Authority (SACAA) ICT Internship 2025

Internship

Location: Midrand, Gauteng

Reference Number SA-308

Application Ends: 17 February 2025

Start Date: 2025

Job Type: Full-time (Internship)

Qualification: National Diploma or Degree in ICT or related field

Job Description

The South African Civil Aviation Authority (SACAA) is offering an ICT Internship for 2025. This internship is designed to provide graduates with practical work experience in the field of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) within the aviation sector. The intern will gain exposure to various ICT functions and contribute to the SACAA's mission of ensuring a safe and secure civil aviation environment.

Internship Overview

The ICT Internship at SACAA is a 12-month program aimed at developing skills and competencies in ICT. The intern will work under the guidance of experienced professionals and will be involved in tasks such as system maintenance, software development, network administration, and IT support. This internship is an excellent opportunity for individuals looking to kickstart their careers in ICT within a dynamic and regulatory environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the maintenance and support of ICT systems and infrastructure.
  • Participate in software development and testing activities.
  • Provide technical support to end-users and resolve IT-related issues.
  • Assist in network administration and cybersecurity tasks.
  • Contribute to the implementation of ICT projects and initiatives.
  • Perform data analysis and generate reports as required.
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure seamless ICT operations.
  • Adhere to SACAA's policies, procedures, and regulatory requirements.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A National Diploma or Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Knowledge of programming languages (e.g., Java, C#, Python) is advantageous.
  • Familiarity with network administration and cybersecurity principles.
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • South African citizenship is required.

Application Process

To apply for the SACAA ICT Internship 2025, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this internship.

Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their applications before the closing date. Please ensure all documentation is included as part of your application.
