Data Capturer Internship - Fidelity Services Group

Internship

Location: Cape Town

Date Posted: 09 February 2025

Application Ends: 13 February 2025

Job Type: Contract (Internship)

Qualification: Matric (Grade 12) or equivalent; basic computer skills

Job Description

Fidelity Services Group is offering a Data Capturer Internship for individuals looking to gain practical experience in data management and administration. The intern will be responsible for capturing, verifying, and maintaining accurate data in the company's systems. This role is ideal for someone who is detail-oriented, organized, and eager to develop skills in data handling and administration.

Internship Overview

The Data Capturer Internship is a 12-month program designed to provide hands-on experience in data capturing and administration. The intern will work closely with the administrative team to ensure the accuracy and integrity of data. This internship offers an excellent opportunity to develop essential skills in data management and gain exposure to a professional work environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Capture and verify data accurately into the company's systems.
  • Maintain and update databases regularly.
  • Ensure data integrity and confidentiality at all times.
  • Assist with data cleaning and validation processes.
  • Generate reports and summaries from captured data as required.
  • Provide administrative support to the team as needed.
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
  • Perform ad hoc tasks as assigned by the supervisor.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • Matric (Grade 12) or equivalent qualification.
  • Basic computer skills, including proficiency in Microsoft Office (Excel, Word).
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy.
  • Good organizational and time management skills.
  • Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • South African citizenship is required.

Application Process

To apply for the Data Capturer Internship at Fidelity Services Group, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this internship.

Should you not be contacted regarding this position within 10 working days from the closing date, please consider your application unsuccessful.
