Northern Cape Provincial Treasury Internship Programme 2025-2027

Internship Programme

Location: Northern Cape, South Africa

Date Posted: 09 February 2025

Application Ends: 21 February 2025

Job Type: Full-time (Internship Programme)

Number of Posts: 20

Job Description

The Northern Cape Provincial Treasury is offering an Internship Programme for 2025-2027, aimed at providing graduates with practical work experience in the public finance sector. This programme is designed to equip participants with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their careers while contributing to the effective management of public resources.

Programme Overview

The Internship Programme is a 24-month initiative that offers participants exposure to various aspects of public finance, including budgeting, accounting, supply chain management, and financial reporting. Interns will be placed in different departments within the Northern Cape Provincial Treasury to gain hands-on experience and develop professional competencies.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the preparation and monitoring of provincial budgets.
  • Support the implementation of financial policies and procedures.
  • Participate in the procurement and supply chain management processes.
  • Contribute to the preparation of financial reports and statements.
  • Engage in data analysis and research to support decision-making.
  • Collaborate with team members to achieve departmental objectives.
  • Adhere to all legislative and regulatory requirements.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A Bachelor's Degree or National Diploma in Finance, Accounting, Economics, Supply Chain Management, or a related field.
  • Strong academic performance and a passion for public finance.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications (e.g., Excel, Word).
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • South African citizenship.
  • No prior work experience required.

Application Process

To apply for the Northern Cape Provincial Treasury Internship Programme 2025-2027, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this programme.
