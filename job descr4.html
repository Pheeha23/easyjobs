Customer Service Clerk - Absa Bank

Job Type: Full-time

Location: Hybrid(Verulam)

Date Posted: 09 February 2025

Application Ends: 13 February 2025

Job Requisition ID: R-15971718

Job Description

Absa Bank is seeking a dedicated and customer-focused Customer Service Clerk to join their team. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing exceptional service to customers, addressing inquiries, and ensuring a positive banking experience. This role requires excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and a passion for helping others.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist customers with account inquiries, transactions, and banking services.
  • Resolve customer complaints and issues in a timely and professional manner.
  • Provide information about Absa Bank's products and services.
  • Process customer requests, such as account openings, closures, and updates.
  • Maintain accurate records of customer interactions and transactions.
  • Collaborate with other team members to achieve customer service goals.
  • Ensure compliance with banking regulations and company policies.
  • Identify opportunities to upsell or cross-sell banking products.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A Matric certificate or equivalent qualification.
  • A minimum of [Insert Years of Experience from the Page] years of experience in customer service, preferably in the banking or financial sector.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong problem-solving and conflict-resolution abilities.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office applications (e.g., Word, Excel).
  • Knowledge of banking systems and processes is an advantage.
  • South African citizenship.

Application Process

To apply for the Customer Service Clerk position at Absa Bank, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this position.
