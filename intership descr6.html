Engineering (Mechatronics) Graduate Programme - Ford Motor Company of South Africa

Graduate Programme

Location: Struanway Road, Port Elizabeth, EC, 6001, ZA

Date Posted: 09 February 2025

Application Ends: 14 February 2025

Job Type: Full-time (Graduate Programme)

Qualification: Bachelor's Degree in Mechatronics Engineering or related field

Job Description

Ford Motor Company of South Africa is offering an Engineering (Mechatronics) Graduate Programme for recent graduates. This programme is designed to provide graduates with hands-on experience in mechatronics engineering within the automotive industry. The successful candidate will work on innovative projects, gain exposure to cutting-edge technologies, and contribute to the development of world-class vehicles.

Programme Overview

The Engineering (Mechatronics) Graduate Programme is a 24-month programme aimed at developing future engineering leaders. The graduate will be exposed to various aspects of mechatronics engineering, including robotics, automation, control systems, and vehicle dynamics. This programme offers a unique opportunity to gain industry-specific knowledge and develop essential engineering skills in a global automotive company.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in the design, development, and testing of mechatronic systems.
  • Participate in the integration of mechanical, electrical, and software components.
  • Support the implementation of automation and robotics solutions.
  • Conduct research and analysis to improve system performance and efficiency.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to achieve project objectives.
  • Prepare technical reports, presentations, and documentation.
  • Adhere to safety and quality standards in all engineering activities.
  • Participate in training sessions, workshops, and development programmes.

Qualifications

Basic Requirements:

  • A Bachelor's Degree in Mechatronics Engineering or a related field.
  • Strong academic performance and a passion for engineering.
  • Knowledge of robotics, automation, and control systems.
  • Proficiency in engineering software tools (e.g., MATLAB, CAD).
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Strong communication and teamwork abilities.
  • South African citizenship is required.

Application Process

To apply for the Engineering (Mechatronics) Graduate Programme at Ford Motor Company of South Africa, please submit the following documents:

  • A detailed CV.
  • A certified copy of your ID.
  • Certified copies of your academic qualifications.
  • A motivational letter explaining why you are the ideal candidate for this programme.
