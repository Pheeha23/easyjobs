Internship

Location: Pretoria, Gauteng

Date Posted: January 17, 2025

Programme Overview

The Department of Human Settlements is inviting applications for their Internship Programme 2025 – 2027, a 24-month initiative aimed at providing unemployed graduates with valuable work experience in various fields.

Application Details

Application Process

Submit applications electronically to DoHS@tananatosolutions.co.za.

  • Use the Z83 form (effective from 01 January 2021), available from the Department of Public Service and Administration website or the National Department of Human Settlements.
  • Include a CV only and ensure the correct reference number appears on the form.

Additional Information

  • Applicants must be South African citizens, unemployed graduates, and must not have participated in any prior internship program.
  • Candidates should be available for interviews as determined by the department. All applications will undergo verification processes.
  • Persons with disabilities are encouraged to apply.
